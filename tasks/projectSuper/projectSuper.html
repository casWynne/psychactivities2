How to use this page

This page helps you explore dissertation topics and find a supervisor whose research fits what you’re curious about. You can browse in two ways:

  • Staff — explore each academic’s areas of supervision and the topics they’re offering.
  • Topics — browse individual dissertation topics one-by-one, with the supervisor shown on each.

Open any card to read the details and see example directions you could take.

When something clicks, use Questions? Email me to contact the supervisor, or hit Interested in this topic to register your interest (need more information to make this more detailed and clear).
