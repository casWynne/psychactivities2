Research Projects Directory

Explore staff projects and find inspiration for your dissertation.

How to use this page

This page helps you explore dissertation topics and identify a supervisor whose research aligns with your interests. You can browse the information in two ways:

  • Staff — explore each academic’s areas of supervision and the topics they are offering.
  • Topics — browse individual dissertation topics one by one, with the supervising staff member shown on each.

Click on any card to expand it and view project details, including example directions you could take.

Search and filter
Use the search box or keyword filter to narrow results to areas that interest you. You can clear these at any time using the ✕ buttons.

Save items to review later
You can save staff members and/or individual topics using the star button. Saving a staff member highlights all of their topics, and you can still unsave individual topics if they are not of interest. Saved items are highlighted so they are easy to return to.

View saved items only
Use the Saved toggle to show only the staff or topics you have saved. A counter shows how many are currently saved, and you can clear them at any time.

Print or save your shortlist
Use the Print / Save button to generate a clean, printable version of the staff or topics currently shown. This can be useful for discussion or planning.

When you find a topic you are interested in, use Questions? Email me to contact the supervisor, or select Interested in this topic to register your interest.

Take time to explore the full range of topics that interest you. Try not to base your choice only on a preferred member of staff, as supervisors have limited capacity and allocations are made based on topic fit.
