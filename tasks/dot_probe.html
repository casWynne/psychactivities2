Dot Probe Task: Animals vs. Objects

Task Description

Definition: The Dot Probe Task measures attentional biases by presenting two stimuli and requiring a quick response to a dot appearing in the location of one of the stimuli.

Instructions: Focus on the center of the screen. Two images will appear, one of a cake and one of a snake. When the dot appears, press the left or right arrow key corresponding to the location of the dot as quickly and accurately as possible.

Press the 'Start Task' button below to begin. After completing all trials, your results will be displayed.
